Lesbian and Gay Liberation in Canada

The Lesbian and Gay Liberation in Canada (LGLC) project brings together accounts of over 34,000 events, people, and places that led to the creation of gay political consciousness in Canada. Explore the movement, its politics, its victories, and its challenges through the LGLC database and contextual essays.
